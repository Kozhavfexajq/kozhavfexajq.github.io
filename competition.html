Kozhavfexajq's Piano Competition

About

This is Kozhavfexajq's first Piano Competition.
A composition competition of piano.

Once the competition gets enough participants, Brackets and a theme for round 1 will be released on this website.
Brackets will be randomized.

There will be a deadline for each round of the competition, and the scores are due at 23:59 UCT on that day.
2 days after the deadline, the next round of the competition will be released.

Join: Comment "Join" or something similar on the score.

Scoring criteria will be released with the themes.
