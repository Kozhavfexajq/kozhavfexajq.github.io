Scores must be submitted by Thursday March 5th at 23:59 UTC/GMT

Round I:

Theme: In the Style of a Classical Composer
For this round, you will be writing a song in the style of a classical composer.
This can be any composer that you choose, as long as they are classical
i.e., Schumann would work, Swift wouldn't. They can be from any time period, from Medieval to Contemporary.
A few composers will be removed:

Paganini

Sorabji

Cage

Yourself

Rules:

Solo piano only

Must choose a classical composer, and have it stated in the title, description, or subtitle of your score.

Must be composed by yourself, and be original.

Scoring Criteria:

30pts: Sounding like the composer you chose

20pts: Sounding good

20pts: Good use of compositional devices
